What we offer ?

We offer a variety of activities for all tastes and ages, ranging from discovering the cultural and historical heritage of the city to water and land sports. We are a team of enthusiasts who seek to share our love for Marseille with you by offering you exclusive and authentic activities. Our experienced local guides will accompany you throughout the activity to show you the best places in Marseille and give you interesting anecdotes about the city. We are committed to providing quality activities, with an emphasis on safety, comfort and guest satisfaction. We look forward to welcoming you and providing you with an unforgettable experience in Marseille.
